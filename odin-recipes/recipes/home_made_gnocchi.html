Homemade gnocchi

Gnoochi is a type of pasta made of potato and parmesan. It is a perfect starter, dinner or something to elevate a salad.

Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 2 pieces of egg yolk
  • 4 tablespoons of shredded parmesan
  • 150g of fine pasta flour
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons of pepper
  • 0.25 teaspoons of muscat
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

How to make gnocchi

  • Peal the potatoes and boil them in unsalted water. Pour off the water and put the pot back on the heater. Let the water evaporate from the bottom of the pot. Let the potatoes cool on the bench until they have cooled a bit.
  • Shred the potatoes such that they have a fine consistency.
  • Make a hole in the middle of the potato pile. Put egg yolk and shredded parmesan in the hole.
  • Mix flour, salt, pepper and muscat, and add it to the egg yolk and parmesan.
  • Mit it all together with your hands. The dough will be quite loose at the start. Add some flour until the dough has a firm and flexible consistency.
  • Cut the dough into smaller pieces which you flatten out. Cut the dough into smaller pieces.
  • Use a fork and press it down onto the small pieces of dough to get a nice gnocchi pattern. Add some flour.
  • Boil water in a big pot. Add salt and boil the gnocchi until they float on top of the water. Now the gnocchi are finished boiling. Don't boil too many gnocchi at once. Collect the gnocchi and pour some olive oil on top of them.
