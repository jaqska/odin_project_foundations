Homemade cauliflower soup

Hot cauliflower is a perfect dish for cold autumn days.

Ingredients

    The soup:

  • 1 cauliflower
  • 0.5 fine-cut onion
  • 8 dl of water
  • 2 pieces of chicken stock
  • 2 dl of milk

    • Topping:

  • 6 slices of bacon cut into fine pieces
  • 2 pieces of spring onion, in thin slices
  • 1 piece of baguette

How to make the soup

  1. Clean the cauliflower and cut it into smaller pieces. Have the cauliflower, onion, water, chicken stock and milk into a pot. Let it boil until the cauliflower is tender.
  2. Take out some of the cauliflower pieces from the soup, and pour the rest into a blender. Let the content blend until the soup has an even consistency. Taste with some salt and pepper.
  3. Fry the bacon until it is crisp. Add the spring onions and let it cook for 1 minute until the spring onion is tender.
  4. Put the pieces of cauliflower into bowls and pour over the soup. Add bacon and spring onion.
